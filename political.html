End Political Killings in Osun, Human Rights Lawyer Petitions Inspector General of Police

Published: June 2026 | Osogbo

OSOGBO — A prominent legal practitioner and human rights advocate has formally petitioned the Inspector General of Police (IGP), urging immediate and decisive action to halt the rising wave of politically motivated killings in Osun State.

The legal practitioner expressed deep concern over the escalating violence ahead of upcoming political cycles, noting that the state, traditionally known for its relative peace, is fast becoming a hotbed for targeted assassinations and thuggery.

A Call for Urgent Intervention

In the petition forwarded to the police headquarters, the lawyer highlighted several recent unresolved murder cases involving political figures and party loyalists. The petition stressed that the failure of local law enforcement to thoroughly investigate these crimes and bring the perpetrators to justice has safely emboldened criminal elements.

"We cannot fold our arms and watch Osun slide into a state of political anarchy. The lives of citizens should not be the price paid for political power. We are calling on the Inspector General of Police to deploy a special task force to investigate these killings and ensure that both the executioners and their sponsors face the full wrath of the law."

The lawyer further urged political parties to caution their supporters and embrace peace, reminding them that democracy thrives on the marketplace of ideas, not on violence.

The police high command has reportedly acknowledged receipt of the petition, promising a thorough review of the security situation in the state to ensure the safety of all residents.
