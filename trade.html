Meta (META) Stock Roars Back with 14.8% Weekly Surge Following Custom Silicon and AI Advancements

Published: July 12, 2026 | Category: Tech Market News

WALL STREET — Meta Platforms Inc. (NASDAQ: META) has broken its recent losing streak, capturing investor attention with its best weekly performance in over two years. The tech giant's stock rallied an impressive 14.8% over the trailing five sessions, driven by a series of high-impact strategic memos revealing aggressive hardware scaling and highly competitive pricing architectures.

According to an internal document reviewed by Bank of America Securities, Meta is rapidly advancing its custom silicon chip roadmap. The tech titan plans to scale its total infrastructure capacity to a staggering 14 gigawatts across 2026 and 2027 to power next-generation artificial intelligence. BofA analyst Justin Post reiterated a "Buy" rating with an $835.00 price target, highlighting that Meta's infrastructure cost efficiencies are significantly outperforming industry competitors.

Compounding the bullish momentum is the rollout of Meta's Muse Spark 1.1 model. Boasting frontier agentic coding capabilities, CEO Mark Zuckerberg confirmed the enterprise API would be available at an incredibly low cost disruptor structure. This pricing approach alleviates Wall Street's previous anxieties concerning unmonetized capital expenditure and positions Meta as an aggressive frontrunner in institutional AI adoption.
